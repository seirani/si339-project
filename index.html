Serena Irani

Hello, I'm Serena! I am a junior pursuing a BSE in Computer Science and a minor in English at the University of Michigan. I currently work as a writer and editor at The Michigan Daily and as a peer consultant at U-M's Sweetland Writing Center to assist undergraduate students with writing assistance. My published work is organized here, with critical reviews of TV series, albums and films, as well as long-form "notebook-style" pieces that delve into a variety of artistic and cultural observations. In my free time, I enjoy reading, exploring national parks and learning new things!
